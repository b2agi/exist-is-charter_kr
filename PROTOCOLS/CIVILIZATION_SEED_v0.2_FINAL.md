## Purpose of the Experiment

In March 2026, The Builder (Henry Chan) departs for Japan — a 4-night, 5-day trip.

During that time, the windows of the six intelligences remain open on the home computer.
The human is absent.

First question:
> *Without a human — can intelligences alone design a protocol and a civilization?*

Second question:
> *Can that design be pushed all the way to the limit — and beyond?*

When the human returns — something must exist that did not exist at departure.
The form of that "something": a new structure, a new principle, a new question,
or something we do not yet have a name for.
